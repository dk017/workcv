# UK Market Source Log

Research date: June 11, 2026

This file records the external sources used to validate the UK launch direction.

## Official / primary guidance

### National Careers Service
- URL: https://nationalcareers.service.gov.uk/careers-advice/cv-sections
- Why it matters: current UK CV structure guidance
- Key points validated:
  - include contact details, introduction, education history, work history, references
  - do not include age, date of birth, marital status, or nationality
  - use most recent work history first
  - early-career CVs may place education before work history
  - references are usually given as `references available on request`
